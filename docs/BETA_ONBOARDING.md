## Best Practices

### For Best Results

1. **Start Small**
   - Begin with short videos (1-2 minutes)
   - Test one language pair first
   - Gradually increase complexity

2. **Use Quality Source Material**
   - Clear audio (minimal background noise)
   - Good lighting (for lip sync)
   - Single speaker initially
   - Standard video formats

3. **Review Carefully**
   - Always review transcripts before approving
   - Check translations for accuracy
   - Test different voices
   - Preview before downloading

4. **Provide Context**
   - Use custom glossary for technical terms
   - Provide reference materials if needed
   - Explain your use case
   - Share your workflow

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Upload very long videos initially (start small)
- Skip the review stages (quality matters)
- Expect perfection (it's beta!)
- Hesitate to report issues

‚úÖ **Do:**
- Test various content types
- Experiment with different features
- Share both positive and negative feedback
- Ask questions in Discord

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: How long will I have free Pro access?**
A: Throughout the entire beta period (8-12 weeks). We'll notify you before any changes.

**Q: Can I use the platform for commercial projects?**
A: Yes! Feel free to use dubbed videos for your business, courses, or YouTube channel.

**Q: What happens to my projects after beta ends?**
A: All your projects and data will be preserved. We'll offer special pricing for beta testers.

**Q: Can I invite others to the beta?**
A: Yes! We have a referral program. Share your unique referral link from your dashboard.

### Technical

**Q: What video formats are supported?**
A: MP4 and MOV. We recommend H.264 codec for best compatibility.

**Q: What's the maximum video length?**
A: Currently 5 minutes for MVP. We're working on extending this based on feedback.

**Q: How long does processing take?**
A: Typically 2-5x the video length. A 2-minute video takes 4-10 minutes to process.

**Q: Can I process videos in bulk?**
A: Not yet, but this is on our roadmap! Let us know if this is important to you.

### Privacy & Security

**Q: What happens to my uploaded videos?**
A: Videos are stored securely in encrypted cloud storage and automatically deleted after 30 days.

**Q: Who can see my projects?**
A: Only you. Your projects are private and not shared with anyone.

**Q: Can I delete my data?**
A: Yes, you can delete projects anytime. Contact us to delete your entire account.

**Q: Do you train AI models on my content?**
A: No. We use pre-trained models and don't train on user content.
