# Beta Program Kickoff Webinar Plan

## Webinar Overview

**Title:** AI Video Dubbing Platform - Beta Kickoff & Training

**Duration:** 60 minutes (45 min presentation + 15 min Q&A)

**Format:** Live webinar via Zoom/Google Meet

**Recording:** Yes, shared with all beta testers

**Target Audience:** All beta testers (50-100 participants)

## Objectives

1. Welcome beta testers and build excitement
2. Demonstrate platform capabilities
3. Provide hands-on training
4. Set expectations for beta program
5. Gather initial feedback and questions
6. Build community among testers

## Agenda

### Pre-Webinar (15 minutes before)
- Open Zoom room early
- Play intro music
- Display welcome slide with instructions
- Test audio/video with early joiners
- Share links in chat (Discord, docs, etc.)

### Introduction (5 minutes)
**Presenter:** CEO/Founder or Product Lead

**Content:**
- Welcome and thank you
- Brief company introduction
- Vision for AI video dubbing
- Why we're doing a beta program
- Excitement about having them involved

**Key Messages:**
- "You're not just testers, you're co-creators"
- "Your feedback will directly shape the product"
- "We're building this FOR you, WITH you"

### Beta Program Overview (5 minutes)
**Presenter:** Beta Program Manager

**Content:**
- Program duration and timeline
- What beta testers get (benefits)
- What we're asking from testers
- Communication channels
- Support resources
- Recognition and rewards

**Slides:**
1. Beta Timeline
2. Your Benefits
3. Your Commitment
4. How to Get Help
5. Recognition Program

### Platform Demo (20 minutes)
**Presenter:** Product Manager or Lead Developer

**Content:**
Live demonstration of the platform from start to finish

**Demo Flow:**

1. **Dashboard Overview (2 min)**
   - Navigation
   - Project list
   - Account settings
   - Support access

2. **Creating a Project (3 min)**
   - Click "New Project"
   - Name and configure
   - Language selection
   - Voice options overview

3. **Video Upload (2 min)**
   - Drag and drop demo
   - File validation
   - Upload progress
   - What happens next

4. **Processing Pipeline (5 min)**
   - Real-time progress tracking
   - Stage explanations:
     * Speech-to-Text + Diarization
     * Machine Translation
     * Voice Generation
     * Lip Synchronization
   - Estimated timing
   - WebSocket updates

5. **Transcript Review (3 min)**
   - Transcript editor interface
   - Editing capabilities
   - Speaker labels
   - Timestamp alignment
   - Approval process

6. **Translation Review (2 min)**
   - Side-by-side editor
   - Making edits
   - Custom glossary
   - Approval

7. **Final Output (3 min)**
   - Video preview
   - Download options
   - Quality comparison
   - Sharing capabilities

**Demo Tips:**
- Use a pre-prepared video (1-2 minutes)
- Have backup recordings in case of technical issues
- Show both success and how to handle errors
- Highlight unique features vs. competitors

### Key Features Deep Dive (10 minutes)
**Presenter:** Technical Lead

**Content:**

1. **Voice Cloning (3 min)**
   - How it works
   - Recording requirements
   - Quality tips
   - Use cases
   - Live demo of creating a voice clone

2. **Multi-Speaker Support (2 min)**
   - Speaker diarization
   - Voice assignment
   - Maintaining speaker distinction
   - Example video

3. **Custom Glossary (2 min)**
   - What it is
   - When to use it
   - How to create one
   - Impact on translation

4. **Quality Controls (3 min)**
   - Confidence scores
   - Quality flags
   - Manual review points
   - Best practices

### Feedback & Participation (5 minutes)
**Presenter:** Beta Program Manager

**Content:**

1. **How to Provide Feedback**
   - In-app feedback form
   - Weekly surveys
   - Discord discussions
   - Support tickets
   - 1-on-1 interviews

2. **What We're Looking For**
   - Honest opinions (good and bad)
   - Specific examples
   - Use case descriptions
   - Feature requests with context
   - Bug reports with details

3. **Feedback Impact**
   - How we prioritize
   - Transparency in roadmap
   - Monthly updates
   - Recognition for contributions

### Getting Started Challenge (3 minutes)
**Presenter:** Beta Program Manager

**Content:**

**Week 1 Challenge:**
"Process your first video and share your experience!"

**Steps:**
1. Activate your beta access (if not done)
2. Upload and process a 1-2 minute video
3. Share your results in Discord #showcase
4. Complete the "First Impressions" survey

**Incentive:**
- Everyone who completes gets a special badge
- Best showcase wins extended Pro access
- Feedback helps shape Week 2 priorities

### Q&A Session (15 minutes)
**Moderator:** Beta Program Manager
**Panel:** CEO, Product Manager, Technical Lead

**Format:**
- Take questions from chat
- Prioritize common/important questions
- Be honest about limitations
- Note feature requests for follow-up

**Anticipated Questions:**

**Technical:**
- Q: What video formats are supported?
- A: MP4 and MOV with H.264 codec recommended

- Q: How long does processing take?
- A: Typically 2-5x video length, varies by complexity

- Q: Can I process longer videos?
- A: Currently limited to 5 minutes, expanding based on feedback

**Features:**
- Q: What languages are supported?
- A: 20+ languages, full list in documentation

- Q: Can I use my own voice?
- A: Yes! Voice cloning requires 6+ seconds of clear audio

- Q: Is lip sync available for all tiers?
- A: Premium feature, included in your beta Pro access

**Business:**
- Q: What happens after beta ends?
- A: Special pricing for beta testers, details coming soon

- Q: Can I use this commercially?
- A: Yes, all beta testers can use for commercial projects

- Q: Will my data be deleted?
- A: No, all projects preserved. Videos auto-delete after 30 days

**Program:**
- Q: How much time commitment is required?
- A: 2-4 hours per week recommended, flexible

- Q: Can I invite others?
- A: Yes! Referral program available in your dashboard

- Q: How do I get support?
- A: Discord, in-app widget, email - we respond within 24 hours

### Closing (2 minutes)
**Presenter:** CEO/Founder

**Content:**
- Thank you for joining
- Excitement for the journey ahead
- Reminder of next steps
- Final call to action
- Contact information

**Key Messages:**
- "We're in this together"
- "Your voice matters"
- "Let's build something amazing"

**Next Steps:**
1. Check your email for webinar recording and resources
2. Join Discord server
3. Activate your beta access
4. Complete your first project this week
5. Fill out the "First Impressions" survey

## Contingency Plans

### Technical Issues
- **Internet failure:** Co-presenter takes over
- **Demo breaks:** Switch to backup recording
- **Zoom crashes:** Backup Google Meet link ready
- **Audio issues:** Switch to chat-only Q&A

### Low Attendance
- Proceed with webinar for those present
- Emphasize recording availability
- Schedule optional repeat session
- Create condensed video version

### Too Many Questions
- Extend Q&A by 10 minutes if needed
- Create FAQ document from questions
- Schedule follow-up office hours
- Answer remaining questions in Discord
