## Email Best Practices

### Timing
- **Onboarding emails:** Send immediately after action
- **Engagement emails:** Send weekly, same day/time
- **Survey emails:** Send Friday mornings
- **Support emails:** Respond within 24 hours
- **Milestone emails:** Send immediately after achievement

### Personalization
- Always use first name
- Reference their specific activity
- Mention their content type/use case
- Acknowledge their contributions

### Tone
- Friendly and conversational
- Enthusiastic but not pushy
- Appreciative and grateful
- Helpful and supportive

### Structure
- Clear subject line
- Brief introduction
- Scannable content (bullets, short paragraphs)
- Clear call-to-action
- Easy way to respond/get help

### Tracking
- Use UTM parameters for links
- Track open rates
- Monitor click-through rates
- Measure response rates
- A/B test subject lines
